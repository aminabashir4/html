OUR STORY

The journey of Pathway Community Foundation

2018

The Beginning

Pathway Community Foundation was established with a vision to bridge the gap between HBCUs and urban development opportunities.

2020

First Smart Cities Challenge

We launched our inaugural HBCU Smart Cities Challenge, bringing together students from 12 HBCUs to develop innovative solutions for urban challenges.

2022

Expansion

Our programs expanded to include workforce development initiatives, creating pathways for HBCU students to enter smart city careers.

2024

Next Chapter

Today, our foundation continues to build innovative programs transforming communities nationwide through HBCU partnerships.
